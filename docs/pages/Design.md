## Web Design
(title): Web Design for Holland Festival
(link:) https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=XwoyeREdLf4
Simon Fraser University, The School of Interactive Arts and Technology 2023 Fall IAT235 Information Design Prof: Russell Taylor TA: Dennis Limbo, Nafira Waili, Paul Brokenshire
Team: Xinran Han, Jenna Han, Honbete Lee, Josh Chang, Aron Aguilera
Art Direction: Wolfgang Weingart Client: Holland Festival
This is a six-week project. Our project started by exploring the design of posters. Over three weeks, we kept trying and testing, finally choosing one of them as the art direction and designing this website. Our microsite is directed towards users who seek a comprehensive resource on the artists featured at the Holland festival before the festival begins. There is an emphasis on their creative process, artistry, and unique ideas. A secondary goal for users is to support the artist directly, so at the end of our user flow, we have an external link that leads to the artist’s personal website.

## App (VR game) design

Title: Boat
By: Xinran Han, Walter (Qiaohui) Xu, Ethan Allwood, Xinran Han, Ningzhi (Koni) Tai
Course: IAT 445 Immersive Environments
Link: https://www.sfu.ca/siat/showcase/spring-2024-project-showcase/iat-445-boat.html

Description: In this game, the user is a fisherman who accidentally reels in a strange doll during a fishing trip. The player is then trapped in a sudden attack of thick fog with their boat anchored out to sea. Meanwhile, the player realizes that there are more dolls on this boat and they must explore the boat and follow the clues and hints to find every doll on the boat. The truth behind the strange dolls eventually comes to light followed by an unpredictable ending.

This game blends elements of horror and puzzle-solving to deliver a heart-pounding experience that gives players insight into the depths of fear and the complexity of human emotion.
