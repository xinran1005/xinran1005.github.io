## Structure
Here is a list of pages, one item for a seperate page. 
- Homepage
- Video
- Photography
- Design

Every page should have a general footer for contact info. 

Details for each page and the general footer could be found in `/docs/pages`

## Visual Style

- Minimal and editorial
- Monochromatic imagery with selective accent colors
- Generous white space
- Asymmetrical composition
- Simple serif and sans-serif typography
- Image-first presentation
- Subtle motion as part of the visual identity

## Details
- Use i18n
- prefer semantic elements over div
- all videos use `Plyr` player
